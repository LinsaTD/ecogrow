Committed to innovation and precision, we deliver high-quality diamonds for both industrial and commercial applications, powered by a dedicated team of over 165 professionals.

Product Overview

CVD stands for Chemical Vapour Deposition, and is the commonly used name for diamonds grown in a laboratory via this process. Unlike HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature) diamonds, CVD diamonds are grown under controlled conditions in labs. They are increasingly popular in engagement rings and fine jewellery for their ethical sourcing, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness. While they are typically lower quality than HPHT diamonds, they offer great value with reduced environmental impact.
